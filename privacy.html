Privacy Policy

At ProCV & JSON Toolkit, we prioritize your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Processing

All data processing for the JSON Toolkit and Resume Builder happens locally in your browser. We do not store or transmit your sensitive JSON data or personal CV details to our servers.

2. AI Feature

When you use the AI Summary Generator, the job title and experience you provide are sent to Google Gemini API to generate the text. No personal identifying information is stored by us during this process.

3. Cookies

We may use basic cookies or local storage to save your preferences for a better user experience.
